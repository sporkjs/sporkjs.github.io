Rose Garden

Create a website that illustrates the use of parametric equations to draw curves.

Turning it in: To turn in this project, you should place the website in a folder called parametric inside of your class website and link the site from your main class homepage.

Requirements: The site should contain at least these pages:
  • (1 page) A home page with a title and links to the pages listed below. Each page below should include a Spork canvas on which your image is drawn.
  • (1 page) Make a function rose(x,y,r) that draws a rose centered at the point (x,y) with radius r. Modify one of your impressionism assignments to use your rose function.
  • (1 page) Make a grid of parametric curves such as this: I suggest you modify your rose(x,y,r) function to become a new function rose(x,y,r,n,m) that draws the rose defined by x=r*COS(n*t) and y=r*SIN(m*t). You can then vary values of n and m in your grid.
