Charles Howard Hinton was a phenomenal mathematician, born in England in 1853. He earned both a Bachelor's and Master's degree from Oxford University. Hinton then went on to teach at Cheltenham Ladie's School, Uppington School, Princeton University, and even at a school in Japan. He married Mary Everest Boole, daughter of famous mathematician George Boole.

Hinton studied a variety of subjects, becoming somewhat of a "jack of all trades". However, he was most interested in the science of dimensions, and the possibility of a fourth dimension. While some of his theories have been debunked, they were crucial building blocks to future dimensional research.
