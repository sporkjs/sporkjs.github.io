Rotated Text

Faith Hoppen

Math 142, Fall 2013

Assignment: For this project we selected 5 words from a Bible verse and combined them using different sizes and rotations.


Tools: In order to complete this, we used basic JavaScript programming functions and the rotate and translate from the Spork library.

Picture:

Code:

//Faith Hoppen
//Math 142
//Rotated Text

background(182,184,191);

font('30px ar cena');
filltext('Let all bitterness and wrath',50,50);
filltext('and anger and clamor and',55,80);
filltext('slander be put away from',55,110);
filltext('you, along with malice.',70,140);
filltext('Be kind to one another,',70,190);
filltext('tenderhearted,',110,220);
filltext('forgiving one another,',74,250);
filltext('as God in Christ',105,280);
filltext('forgave you.',125,310);

font('20px ar cena');
filltext('Ephesians 4:31-32',250,390);

save(); 
color(102,102,102);                                        //bitterness
translate(10,205);
rotate(270);
font('55px courier');
stroketext('BITTERNESS',-155,50);
restore();

save();   
color(102,102,102);                                      //wrath
font('60px times');
stroketext('WRATH',175,155);
restore();

save();                                         //anger
color(102,102,102);
translate(0,120);
rotate(-55);
font('30px broadway');
stroketext('ANGER',30,115);
restore();

save();                                       //clamor
color(102,102,102);
translate(200,200);
rotate(50);
font('40px bookman old style');
stroketext('CLAMOR',40,10);
restore();

color(102,102,102);                    //slander
font('45px copperplate gothic bold');  
stroketext('SLANDER',70,220);

save();                                        //malice
translate(170,30);
rotate(20);
font('33px, candara');
stroketext('MALICE',0,0);
restore();
