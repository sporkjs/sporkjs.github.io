House

Faith Hoppen

Math 142, Fall 2013

Assignment: In this project, we were asked to draw a house using three different shapes and three different colors.


Tools: In order to complete this, we used basic JavaScript programming functions and the Spork library.

Picture:

Code:

//Faith Hoppen
//Math 142
//House

background(10, 45, 140);           //Sky

arc(300,450,120,135,360);          //Knoll
fillcolor('green');
fill();
arc(112,460,160,135,360);
fillcolor('green');
fill();

rect(80,180,70,125);               //House
fillcolor(145,10,10);
fill();

triangle(80,180,150,180,115,140);  //Roof
fillcolor('orange');
fill();

rect(120,265,20,40);               //Door
fillcolor('white');
fill();

circle(134,286,2);                 //Doorknob
fillcolor('silver');
fill();

color('lightblue');                //Window
rect(91,200,50,15);
fillcolor('lightblue');
fill();
color('blue')
line(116,200,116,215);
line(91,207.5,141,207.5);

color('yellow');                   //Star
line(299,40,302,47,309,47,302,51,305.5,58,299,52,294,58,296,51,290,47,297,47,299,40);
fillcolor('yellow');
fill();
