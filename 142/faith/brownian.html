Brownian Motion

Faith Hoppen

Math 142, Fall 2013

Assignment: In this project, we were asked to write a program that included funtions to take an initial point and from that create another point at a random adjacent point, and to continue the cycle from there.


Tools: In order to complete this, we used the variable, function, loop, and random functions from JavaScript programming and the Spork editor to make it come to life.
