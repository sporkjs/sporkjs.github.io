Scott Draves is a computer programmer famous for developing the software for Electric Sheep, a distributed computing program. He was born in 1968 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Draves reports that his first experience with computers was in 1978 when he was 10 years old; Draves' first personal computer was an Apple //e. He was immediately interesting in the programming aspect of computers and would experiment with code on his Apple. Draves graduated from Brown University with a degree in Math and received his PhD in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh. Draves' programming first took off with Fractal Flames, which preceded Electric Sheep. In 1997 he moved to the San Francisco area and worked with DreamsWorks. Draves currrently lives in New York City with his wife and children and works at Google as a Software Engineer.
