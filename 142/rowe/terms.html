Rift Herald: Jungle monster on the upper side of the map, generally takes up to two players to defeat. Provides a buff for a single member of your team that makes nearby minions stronger.

Dragon: Jungle monster on the bottom side of the map, each time you defeat the dragon you get a different permanent buff, up to the fifth and strongest buff.

Baron: Jungle monster that replaces Rift Herald at 15 minutes. Generally takes the entire team to defeat and provides Rift Herald's buff to your entire team.

Sustain: Anything that helps keep your health up, be it a temporary shield, a heal, or a regeneration effect.

Crowd Control: Any spell effect that prevents a character from acting normally. This can include slowing them down, stunning them in place, preventing them from casting spells, etc.

Summoner Spell: Two long-cooldown spells you pick with your character. Different spells are meta for different positions.

True Damage: A type of damage that will always deal the amount stated, regardless of resistances.

Jungle Camps: Groups of monsters withing the jungle that team members can kill for gold and experience points.

Initiation: A sudden burst of crowd control dealt by a spell to catch your opponents by surprise. Meant to start fights quickly and without warning to ensure a quick and easy fight.

Buff: Any effect on your character that increases a certain stat or gives a new effect.

Debuff: Opposite of Buff.

Burst: High damage dealt by a character meant to kill an enemy in one blow.

Split Push: Taking objectives to get into the enemy base while your team is taking other objectives on another side of the map.
