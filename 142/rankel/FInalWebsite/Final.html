The "need to know" on the types of assistance aniamls

There are three main catagories of working dogs: Service Dogs, Therapy Dogs, and Emotional Support Animals. Each catagory is different than the other and some have more protection under the law. This website is a friendly and helpful compilation of information that I've learned about. All of my sources can be found on the source page with a brief description of each website. Feel free to click on them to learn more on your own time. I hope this page helps educate you on the difference between these three classes of working dogs.
