The Dog Gallery

This is a gallery of handler's dogs. Service dogs can be any kind of dog. These animals help their handler on a day-to-day basis complete tasks that impair their everyday living. Sometimes the need for a service animals is more obvious than others, but a disibility is a disibility and they don't discriminate people. Handlers are people too and their dogs are just dogs when it comes down to it. So treat them with kindness and respect. We're just trying to get through our day.
