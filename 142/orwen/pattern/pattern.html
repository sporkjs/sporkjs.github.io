Loop Picture

While attempting to make something more akin to a flower generator I made this trippy pattern generator instead. I started with an ellipse, the x coordinate is always the same but the y is randomized. I then took the ellipse and used a loop to rotate it and another one to make the other rings of “petals” and used it to shrink it.

Also can occasionally act as an accidental infinite skip button for Pandora if you change the right numbers to lag the browser enough.
