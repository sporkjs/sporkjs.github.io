About this WebQuest:

This WebQuest was originally designed for a sixth grade social studies class. Students should already have learned basic knowledge about the sinking of the Titanic in class. Students should understand where England is on a map in comparison to the United States. Students should already know how to format a paper and what heading you expect to see on all of their papers. Access to an iPad would benefit them in creating their video if they want to use Shadow Puppet or Videolicious. The WebQuest would fit into curriculum that includes history about the 1920s.

Standards Addressed:

Nebraska State Standards:

  • SS 0.4.2 Students will identify historical people, events, ideas, and symbols.
  • SS 0.4.3.a Understand that two people can tell a story about the same event and share different details
