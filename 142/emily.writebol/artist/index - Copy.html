"I'm a code junky. I write it for my own pleasure."

Andy Lomas is an artist with an extensive resume that stretches his artistic touch across multiple digital platforms and media. He is known for his work in many major films, but also works on his own personal art series that explore organic and natural forms. Lomas works with videos, still images, and programing while using mathematical concepts and algorithms.

Born in England 1967, Lomas developed a strong background in and an undergraduate in Mathematics which he would use to bring his digital creations to life. Lomas became inspired by organic forms and nature, mainly from Scuba Diving and looking at the Coral shapes.

Lomas has credits in a lot of film and television for his work on graphics and special effects and has received many awards, notably: two Emmys and a Lumen Prize. He is evidence that a passion for both art and math can lead to lots of visually exciting and intriguing creations.
