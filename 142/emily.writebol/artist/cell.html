Cellular Forms is the winner of The Lumen prize for Lomas, and is another video generated by algorithms. This project, like a lot of Lomas's other works, explores more organic and lively forms. When discussing the work, Lomas says: "The aim is to create structures emergently: exploring generic similarities between many different forms in nature rather than recreating any particular organism, in the process exploring universal archetypal forms that can come from growth processes rather than top-down externally engineered design."
